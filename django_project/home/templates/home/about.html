About The Site

The goal of this site is to extend the brotherhood of Sigma Chi at UVa through the power of networking. We have compiled information from both undergraduate brothers and alumni to allow us to learn from one another. For young brothers, we can learn from the real world success our alumni in their careers. For alumni, they serve as valuable mentors for younger members and promote the success of our chapter in the workplace.

The main functionality of the site allows registered members to view current and former members as well as search for members using keywords like name, current occupation, school year, etc as seen below.
